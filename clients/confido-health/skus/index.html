§ How they fit together

The funnel: diagnostic → flagship → playbook + pricing → enterprise pursuit.

SKU 01 is the front door. Most clients land here, get the diagnostic, then convert into SKU 02 (the 90-Day Plan). SKU 03 (Playbook) and SKU 05 (Pricing) bundle on top of SKU 02 with discounts. SKU 04 (Enterprise Pursuit) is its own track — the premium add-on for organizations like Confido that have the proof to chase enterprise IDNs.

For Confido specifically, the highest-leverage Bayshore engagement is SKU 02 ($40K) + SKU 05 bundled ($10K) = $50K total — covers the GTM rebuild + pricing audit running parallel. SKU 04 (Enterprise Pursuit) is the natural next conversation after York Hospital becomes 2-3 enterprise references.
