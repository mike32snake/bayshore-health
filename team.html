Adam Teitelman

Founder · Healthcare BD & Revenue

Healthcare operator and investor with fifteen-plus years across value-based care, clinical care-in-the-home, and AI-enabled health tech. Senior Director, Providers & Payors at myLaurel, where he runs an operating role inside a high-growth care-in-the-home company alongside the advisory practice.

Active GTM advisor to twenty-plus healthtech companies — from pre-seed to Series C — covering hospital and IDN motion design, payer pursuit strategy, value-based-care contracting, and clinical-stakeholder mapping. The kind of work most consultancies promise on a deck and Adam has actually run as an in-seat operator.
